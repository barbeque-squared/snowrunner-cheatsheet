Tennessee = ` Stage 2 Finale
  1. Preparation: put any vehicle at the place of the second checkpoint
  2. Start a co-op session and have the other player sit in the vehicle at the second checkpoint
  3. Start the mission
  4. Drive from start to finish by going around the pit. The other vehicle will get the second checkpoint

To get the second vehicle in, try lowering it with a crane from the road at the finish.

To get the second vehicle out, get it close enough to the starting point that you can winch it out with another truck. The water is shallow for the most part.
